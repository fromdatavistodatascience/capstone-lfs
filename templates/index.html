About Enzy

The goal of Enzy is to anticipate whether a customer is going to make an international transaction in a given time period and notify them if the exchange rate drops below the usual levels.
This creates a win-win scenario for both the customer and the bank. The former will save money on their transaction and the bank will benefit by having the user make the transaction through their platform.
